# Simulated S&P 500 Options Portfolio

## Objective

Develop a systematic options strategy with defined risk limits and Greeks-based portfolio management.

---

## Investment Question

Can disciplined options positioning generate attractive risk-adjusted returns while maintaining controlled downside exposure?

---

## Strategy Framework

The portfolio incorporates:

- Black-Scholes pricing
- Greeks calculations
- Position sizing
- Risk limits
- Rolling rules
- Premium management

---

## Risk Management

The portfolio tracks:

- Delta Exposure
- Gamma Exposure
- Vega Exposure
- Theta Decay
- Portfolio VaR

---

## Portfolio Controls

- Position Limits
- Maximum Drawdown Rules
- Rolling Thresholds
- Concentration Limits
- Premium Exposure Limits
